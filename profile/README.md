Mantalus is an advanced cloud consultancy headquartered in Melbourne, Australia, specialising in the design and delivery of solutions on **Amazon Web Services (AWS)**, **Google Cloud Platform (GCP)**, and **Microsoft Azure**.

Founded by technologists with decades of industry experience, our team has helped 100+ businesses globally — from fast-growing Australian startups to multinational enterprises — achieve measurable, lasting outcomes in their cloud journeys.
